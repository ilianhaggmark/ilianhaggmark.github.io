ヘッグマルク　イリアン

Researcher in X-ray imaging

I'm a researcher at The University of Tokyo working with X-ray imaging. My work focuses on phase-contrast methods for improved image quality of biological samples.

The aim of this simple homepage is to briefly present me and my research, but also to serve as a sandbox for HTML/CSS.

Research

Currently I'm doing research under a JSPS Fellowship in the research group of Prof. Tekenori Sasaki at The University of Tokyo University Museum (東京大学総合研究博物館).
